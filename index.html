Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Existential Robotics Laboratory, ECE Department and Contextual Robotics Institute, UC San Diego.
My advisor is Prof. Nikolay Atanasov.
I received my M.S. degree from Oregon State University, Corvallis, OR and B.S. degree from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Hanoi, Vietnam.

Research Interests

  • Robotics

    • Exploration, Planning and Control

    • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM)

    • Environment Perception and Representation

    • Robot Dynamics Learning

  • Decision Making under Uncertainty

  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
